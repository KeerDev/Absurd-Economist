Poem 1

Friendly friendly fire
Why have you denied
Relegated me a fortune
And gone, quietly behind
Hurriedly hurriedly summer
Why have you come
Drenched me with sorrow
And stayed, soaring glum
Frantic frantic footsteps
Where are you going
The truth was already killed
And left, in the river flowing
Whisper whisper my forefathers What are you trying to say My thoughts cloud and burden And haunt me, their prey No more will I pay His heed I'm at mercy for your amnesty Pardon me, acquit me Gently gently, am I free?

Regression of Sisyphus

Each month is a boulder,
and I, the statistician,
roll it up the hill with clustered errors.
