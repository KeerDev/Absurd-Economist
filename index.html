Endogenous Thirst Shocks

Endogenous thirst shocks keep ruining my workflow.
A marginal sip and the gradient of ideas flattens.
I write a theorem on the back of a napkin, then misplace the napkin.

Regression of Sisyphus

Each month is a boulder,
and I, the statistician,
roll it up the hill with clustered errors.
